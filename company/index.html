Corporate Philosophy

 

Purpose

 

We aim to develop talent who can thrive anywhere in the world and expand their possibilities.

We aim to empower people to carve out their own futures, regardless of where they were born or their environment.

 

Mission

 

We provide opportunities to learn, work, and grow overseas, and cultivate globally competitive talent.

We have been providing IT education services to IT-related companies in Cebu, Philippines in 2011, Dhaka, Bangladesh in 2014, and Skopje, Macedonia (currently based in Ohrid) in 2017.

We accept people who want to live, work, and thrive overseas, and while working with local and other team members, we cultivate human resources who can thrive anywhere in the world.

 

Value

 

– Taking on the challenge of overcoming national, cultural and language barriers
– Think and act independently
– Learn and grow together
